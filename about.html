SWITCH

Every year, the dancers of Nederlands Dans Theater take complete control and put together an entire evening of dance. Not only do we create new choreographies, we are responsible for the entire production of the event; from ticket sales and marketing to fundraising and organization. The entire revenue of Switch will be donated to a charity fund chosen by the dancers of NDT.
